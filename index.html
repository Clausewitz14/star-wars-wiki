My Favorite Star Wars Characters

Yoda

This is Yoda. He is more than 900 years old and has seen almost all of Star War history He is small in stature, although is respected for his wisdom. He trained thousands of Jedi and is widly regarded as the wisest mentor the Jedi Empire has ever known.

Finn

This is Finn. He was a ex-Stormtrooper but joined the Jedi when a X-Wing Pilot Poe Dameron covinced him to join the Jedi.

Hans Solo

This is Hans Solo. He is a rebel pilot and is one of the most experienced on the Jedi team. He is known for his wisecracks and his powerful spaceship the Millennium Falcon
